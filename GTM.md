# GTM.md

> Go-to-market strategy and launch plan for SpendScope.

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Startup founders (Seed → Series A) who manage 5–50 person engineering teams

**Secondary:** Engineering managers at mid-size companies with AI tool budgets

**Pain point:** They have no idea what their AI tools actually cost per head, and budgets are growing opaque.

---

## Positioning

**Tagline:** "You're probably overspending on AI tools."

**Positioning Statement:**
For startup founders and engineering leaders who use multiple AI tools,
SpendScope is a free AI spend auditor that instantly reveals where you're overpaying,
unlike manual spreadsheet tracking or guessing, SpendScope gives you a shareable, actionable audit in 60 seconds.
